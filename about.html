Welcome to Chukwuma Nnamdi Resume.

Nnamdi Chukwuma, began his educational career at Faith Baptist School, Located at Old Gra, Opposite Holy Rosary Collegue, Where he obtained his first School Leaving Certificate.

He Obtained his West African Examination Council Certificate at Baptist High School Borikiri, Port Harcourt. due to his bad result, he enrolled at Philip Academy, Nnaka, in Mile 1 Diobu to resit his West African Examination Certificate where he came out with flying colours

After his WAEC, He obtained a National Diploma Certificate in Electrical Electronics Engineering fron National Metallurgical Institute, Obosi, Anambra State.

He obtained his Bachalor of Science Certificate and the prestigous University Of Port Harcourt in 2008.
